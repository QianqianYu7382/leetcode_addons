# Privacy Policy for LeetCode Spaced Repetition Manager

**Last Updated:** November 30, 2025

This Chrome Extension is designed to help users manage their LeetCode practice schedule locally.

## Data Collection

**This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) of the user.**

* **No user data leaves your browser.** All problem records (e.g., problem title, URL, solve date, review count) are stored exclusively on your local machine using the Chrome browser's secure `chrome.storage.local` API.
* We do not use analytics, tracking cookies, or any third-party services that collect user information.

## Permissions Usage

All permissions requested are solely for the intended functionality:

* **`storage`:** To store local problem records.
* **Host Permissions (`*://leetcode.com/problems/*`):** To inject the "Mark as Solved" button onto LeetCode pages.
* **`alarms` & `notifications`:** To schedule and display local review reminders.

**Your privacy is 100% protected as your data remains entirely local.**
